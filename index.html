Y-Factor Curve

This page shows the reference curve of the Y-factor. The right column shows 24 different carbon abatement options. If you wish to show only a limited selection of these carbon abatement options, switch off the options you wish to exclude and press the submit button.

On top of the graphs there is a button called 'Toggle level of detail'. When clicking this button, the level of detail of the Y-factor curve will change, from 4 to 12 categories. How these categories have been specified can be found at the homepage
